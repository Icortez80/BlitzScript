BlitzScript, the language of champions!

BlitzScript is targeting beginners, CS students, and football fans who find programming syntax unintuitive and abstract. The problem when first learning a language is understanding keywords, loops, and use of variables. BlitzScript aims to bridge that mental gap with football concepts that most fans already know and understand, creating a more approachable language.

helloWorld.bs

// BlitzScript - your first play
coin_toss
    announce "Hello, World!"
end

FizzBuzz.bs

// FizzBuzz - The Classic
coin_toss
    drive while play  <= 100
        when play flagged_by 3 and 5
                announce "FizzBuzz"
        when play flagged_by 5
                announce "Fizz"
        when play flagged_by 3
                announce "Buzz"
        otherwise
                // This will output the current integer
                announce play
        end
        // Increment the integer by 1
        play holding 1
end
